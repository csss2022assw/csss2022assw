Student Awards

There will be a number of travel and presentation awards for eligible students, which will be sponsored by the Canadian Society of Soil Science (CSSS). Please see the CSSS awards critieria for details. Please indicate during your abstract submission, if you are willing to be considered for student presentation awards.

Beside the CSSS awards, there will be additional student presentation and travel awards, which will be sponsored by the Alberta Soil Science Workshop (ASSW) for eligible students from post-secondary institutions within Alberta. ASSW student presentations will be awarded to the top 3 student presenters from Alberta institutions in both oral and poster categories.

If you are a student from an Alberta institution and you need travel cost assistance, please apply for the ASSW student travel awards by filling out the (and email the form following the instruction within the form).
