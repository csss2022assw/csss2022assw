Sponsors:

Sponsorship Opportunities at the Canadian Society of Soil Science-Alberta Soil Science Workshop Joint Conference 2022

To support the upcoming CSSS-ASSW 2022, we are seeking sponsorships. All sponsors receive the benefit of positioning themselves, in the minds of the public and today’s young scientists from Canada and from around the world, as organizations committed to the responsible management of soils and other natural resources. We invite you to make your commitment as soon as possible. Recognition in the program document requires receipt of your information by 15 April 2022 (please click the "Download sponsorship form" button above to download the sponsorship information form).

Note: The Organizing Committee reserves the right to refuse a sponsorship offer if the committee considers a specific sponsorship inappropriate for this conference.

SPONSORS

PATRON ($4,001 and above)

Benefits include:

• Large sign with company logo (approx. 3’ X 2’) placed at the registration area placed individually
• Two complimentary registrations + two complimentary tickets for the Banquet
• Logo on the website in sponsor patron area and max. 100-word company promo
• Opportunity to have an exhibit present during the conference

PLATINUM ($2,001-$4,000)

Benefits include:

• Large sign with company logo shared with a maximum of two other logos at the registration area
• One complimentary registration + one complimentary ticket for the Banquet
• Logo on the website in sponsor area and max. 50-word company promo
• Opportunity to have an exhibit present during the conference

GOLD ($1,001-$2,000)

Benefits include:

• Sign with company logo shared with ten other logos placed at the registration area
• One complimentary registration
• Logo on the website in sponsor area

SILVER ($501-$1,000)

Benefits include:

• Listing with company logo at the registration area
• Logo on the website in sponsor area

BRONZE ($500 and below)

Benefits include:

• Listing with company logo at the registration area
• Logo on the website in sponsor area

Common benefits at all sponsorship levels

• Acknowledgement in proceedings/book of abstracts, in field tour guidebooks, and during acknowledgment slide presentations
• Sponsorship acknowledged at the Reception/Banquet, and at the beginning of field tours
