Sponsorship Opportunities at the Canadian Society of Soil Science-Alberta Soil Science Workshop Joint Conference 2022

The 2022 annual meetings of the Canadian Society of Soil Science (CSSS)-Alberta Soil Science Workshop (ASSW) (CSSS-ASSW 2022) will be held at the University of Alberta Campus in Edmonton, May 23-27, 2022.

The theme of the CSSS-ASSW 2022 is Soil Science for Sustainable Development.

To support the upcoming CSSS-ASSW 2022, we are seeking sponsorships. The following sections outline the details of benefits to sponsoring organizations relative to contribution levels. All sponsors receive the benefit of positioning themselves, in the minds of the public and today’s young scientists from Canada and from around the world, as organizations committed to the responsible management of soils and other natural resources. We invite you to make your commitment as soon as possible. Recognition in the program document requires receipt of your information by 15 April 2022.

Yours sincerely,

The 2022 CSSS-ASSW Organizing Committee

Note: The Organizing Committee reserves the right to refuse a sponsorship offer if the committee considers a specific sponsorship inappropriate for this conference.

Detailed Benefits of Sponsorship Packages

Common benefits at all sponsorship levels

  • • Acknowledgement in proceedings/book of abstracts, in field tour guidebooks, and during acknowledgment slide presentations
  • • Sponsorship acknowledged at the Reception/Banquet, and at the beginning of field tours
